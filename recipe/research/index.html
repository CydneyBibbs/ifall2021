ACAI POWER HOUSE

This Acai Bowl is the best smoothie bowl! It’s made with Brazilian Acai puree, frozen bananas and blueberries and sweet juice. Then they’re finished with fresh fruit, granola and honey as desired. An all time favorite!

Ingredients

• (1) frozen banana

• (2) 4 ounce packets of pure, unsweetened acai puree

• (3/4) cups of nut, rice, or seed milk

• (2) Medjool dates, pitted


• (1/2) cup of chopped, frozen mangos

• (1/2) cup of frozen blueberries

• (1/2) cup of torn kale leaves

• (1/4) cup of plain full-fat Greek yogurt


• (1) tablespoons of fresh lime juice

• (2) teaspoon of maca powder

• (A) pinch of kosher salt

Toppings Suggestions

• Crushed cereal

• Golden berries

• Toasted coconut flakes

• Goji berries

• Dried figs

• Cocoa nibs

Instructions

Step One

• Place two bowls in freezer to chill them


• Cut off one-quarter of banana and set aside for serving.

Step Two

• Crush the acai packets with a hammer, rolling pin, or heavy skillet.


• Add acai, dates, mango, blueberries, kale, yogurt, lime juice, maca powder, salt, and remaining frozen banana.


• Blend on low speed to combine, then blend on high.


• Blend until mixture is well combined and very thick.

Step Three

• Divide acai mixture between chilled bowls and top with different toppings, including reserved banana.


• Enjoy!
